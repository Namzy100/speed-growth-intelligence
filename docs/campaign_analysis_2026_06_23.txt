=============================================================
SECTION 2: UNDERPERFORMING CAMPAIGNS
=============================================================

1. Meta / Cash Deposit
   Spend: $645.02 | Installs: 12 | CPI: $53.75
   Generated 95,967 impressions and 2,279 clicks but converted only 12 installs. The click-to-install rate is roughly 0.5%, indicating a severe disconnect between the ad creative or audience and the app store conversion. CPI is 15x higher than the best-performing Meta campaign.

2. Meta / Re-engagement - Android
   Spend: $144.42 | Installs: 6 | CPI: $24.07
   Only 6 installs from 25,928 impressions. The campaign name suggests a re-engagement objective, but Adjust attributes it to new installs, creating possible tracking or objective misalignment. CPI is nearly 7x worse than Payday - Android - Broad+.

3. Meta / Re-targeting Campaign Android and iOS
   Spend: $217.74 | Installs: 16 | CPI: $13.61
   Delivered 22,257 impressions and 504 clicks but only 16 installs. Retargeting audiences may be too small or too cold, and blending Android and iOS into one campaign obscures optimization signals.

4. Facebook / iOS - App Promotion Campaign
   Adjust eCPI: $8.57 | Meta CPI: $6.93 | Spend: $1,379.75 | Installs (Adjust): 161
   Both sources confirm weak performance. At $8.57 on Adjust versus $3.46 for the top Facebook campaign, iOS delivery costs are more than twice as expensive for roughly one-tenth the volume.

=============================================================
SECTION 3: RECOMMENDED ACTIONS PER UNDERPERFORMER
=============================================================

1. Meta / Cash Deposit
   Action: Pause immediately and reallocate its $645 budget to Payday - Android - Broad+.
   Rationale: A $53.75 CPI against a portfolio benchmark of $3.46 on the same platform is indefensible; the creative or audience is fundamentally broken and burning budget daily.

2. Meta / Re-engagement - Android
   Action: Audit the campaign objective and Adjust event mapping before spending further; pause until tracking is confirmed correct.
   Rationale: A re-engagement campaign driving only 6 attributed installs at $24.07 suggests the campaign may be hitting already-active users or firing the wrong conversion event, making optimization impossible.

3. Meta / Re-targeting Campaign Android and iOS
   Action: Split into separate Android and iOS campaigns, each with a dedicated audience list, and cut combined budget by 50% during the test.
   Rationale: Blending platforms at $13.61 CPI suppresses the algorithm's ability to optimize; separating them mirrors the strategy that made Payday - Android - Broad+ the top performer at $3.46.

4. Facebook / iOS - App Promotion Campaign
   Action: Reduce budget by 60% and redirect savings to Apple Search Ads / US - Brand - Exact, which delivers iOS installs at $3.38 versus Meta iOS at $8.57.
   Rationale: Apple Search Ads is already proving a more cost-efficient iOS channel by 2.5x; shift the marginal iOS dollar to the channel with demonstrated performance.
