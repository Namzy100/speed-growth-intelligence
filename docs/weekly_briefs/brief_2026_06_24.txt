The three most important findings this week are the dominance of organic installs representing 67 percent of total volume, Facebook's emergence as the most cost-efficient paid channel at an eCPI of $3.87, and the sustained strength of brand campaigns on Google and Apple driving meaningful scale at manageable unit economics. Organic's 27,542 installs over the past thirty days signals genuine product-market fit, and the Partnership channel delivered another 1,044 installs led by Thndr and Fumb without any media spend, meaning earned distribution channels are working at scale.

Facebook is performing most efficiently right now, delivering 1,746 installs at $3.87 eCPI, notably lower than Google Ads at $5.00 and even Apple at $3.91. The Payday Android Broad+ campaign alone brought in 1,589 installs at just $3.47, meaning the remittance positioning is resonating with audiences who are price-sensitive and conversion-ready. The creative or targeting refinement inside that campaign should become the template for future tests across demographics and geos, particularly for the Mexico and Brazil influencer markets where remittance volume is high and word-of-mouth can amplify paid spend.

Google Ads needs a budget review despite solid volume because its $5.00 blended eCPI is nearly 30 percent higher than Facebook and 28 percent higher than Apple, yet it consumed the largest share of paid spend at over $27,000 in the period. The New Android Brand US campaign generated 3,492 installs at $5.12 each, which is acceptable for brand defense but expensive for growth, and the XAUT offers campaign delivered 1,887 installs at $4.92 with unclear downstream conversion to the core segments of remittance or iGaming. If XAUT is attracting crypto-curious users who churn quickly, the unit economics won't hold at scale.

The specific recommended action is to shift 20 percent of the Google Ads budget into Facebook and run three creative variants of the Payday broad targeting campaign tailored to iGaming users and crypto-curious mainstream segments, using the same offer structure that drove the $3.47 eCPI. This hedge maintains Google's brand presence while testing whether Facebook's efficiency can scale beyond remittance senders, and it diversifies creative risk across the three core hooks before the summer acquisition window closes.

D1 retention is flat, with the recent seven-day average at 25.4 percent versus 25.8 percent in the prior period, a delta of negative 0.4 percent that sits within normal variance. The data shows no structural drop, and day-to-day swings between 23 and 31 percent suggest cohort composition variability rather than product degradation. Flat retention during a period of high organic growth is a neutral signal, meaning onboarding and core engagement loops are holding steady but not improving as volume scales, which will matter if paid acquisition expands rapidly in Q3.

Robinhood is leaning heavily into premium membership perks like Gold APY and advanced trading tools such as the Legend platform, targeting active traders with sophisticated features rather than beginners, while Crypto.com is doubling down on gamification through missions, mystery boxes, and card cashback rewards with zero-fee promotional hooks to drive engagement. Neither competitor is positioning crypto simplicity as a hero message, creating a clear lane for Speed to own the crypto-curious mainstream segment with ease-of-use and Lightning speed as differentiators.

Germany ranks as the top EU market to enter first with 1,657 organic installs already showing demand that Speed has not yet activated, a deep crypto-curious segment, and a single EMI license covering future EU expansion from within the regulatory perimeter. The UK follows due to the highest-volume India and Nigeria remittance corridors in Europe plus the most developed iGaming market, though it requires a separate post-Brexit regulatory path. Portugal rounds out the top three with unmatched Brazil corridor concentration on a per-capita basis, offering a dense word-of-mouth launch opportunity among Brazilian nationals before broader scaling.
