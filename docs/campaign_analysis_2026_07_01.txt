2. UNDERPERFORMING CAMPAIGNS
------------------------------

Cash Deposit (Meta)
Spend: $1,051.36 | Installs: 13 | CPI: $80.87
Generating 3,228 clicks against 136,927 impressions but converting almost none to installs. Click-to-install rate is catastrophically low. Either the post-click landing experience is broken, the audience is mismatched, or install tracking is firing incorrectly.

Re-targeting Campaign Android & iOS (Meta)
Spend: $379.85 | Installs: 20 | CPI: $18.99
50,346 impressions and 911 clicks yielding only 20 installs. A retargeting campaign should convert at a premium since the audience is warm. At $18.99 CPI it is running at 6x the efficiency of Payday - Android - Broad+.

Re-engagement - Android (Meta)
Spend: $144.42 | Installs: 6 | CPI: $24.07
25,928 impressions and 180 clicks for 6 installs. Lowest absolute install count among active paid Meta campaigns. CPI is 7.6x the Payday benchmark.

Payday android Video (Adjust)
Installs: 56 | Cost: $385.19 | eCPI: $6.88
CPI is 2x the comparable Payday - Android - Broad+ campaign ($3.31). Video creative is not matching the efficiency of the broad creative despite similar targeting intent.


3. RECOMMENDED ACTION PER UNDERPERFORMER
------------------------------------------

Cash Deposit: Pause immediately and audit the post-click funnel.
Rationale: $1,051 spent for 13 installs at $80.87 CPI signals a broken link between ad click and app store — verify deep link and store redirect before spending another dollar.

Re-targeting Campaign Android & iOS: Cut budget by 75% and refresh the creative audience segment.
Rationale: 911 clicks at $18.99 CPI means the retargeted audience is likely exhausted or too broad — reduce spend to a test floor and swap in a tighter custom audience from recent non-activating installs.

Re-engagement - Android: Pause and redirect the $144 weekly budget to Payday - Android - Broad+.
Rationale: 6 installs for $144 is statistically insufficient to optimize; the Payday campaign is delivering installs at $3.15, making reallocation an immediate efficiency gain.

Payday android Video: Swap creative; test a static or carousel format against the same audience.
Rationale: The video format costs $6.88 per install versus $3.31 for the broad static campaign — same targeting context, so the creative format is the variable to change.
