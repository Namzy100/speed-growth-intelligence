SECTION 2 — UNDERPERFORMING CAMPAIGNS
---------------------------------------

1. Meta / Cash Deposit
   Spend: $645 | Installs: 12 | CPI: $53.75
   Worst CPI in the entire dataset by a large margin — 15x worse than Payday Broad+. Generated 2,279 clicks against only 12 installs, indicating a severe drop-off between click and install. Either the landing/store page experience is broken for this audience or the creative attracts low-intent users.

2. Meta / Re-engagement - Android
   Spend: $144 | Installs: 6 | CPI: $24.07
   Six installs from a re-engagement campaign suggests the audience is either too narrow, incorrectly segmented, or the "re-engagement" objective is firing installs against already-active users. Poor return for even a modest budget.

3. Meta / Re-targeting Campaign Android & iOS
   Spend: $218 | Installs: 16 | CPI: $13.61
   More than 4x the CPI of Payday Broad+. High impression volume (22,257) with only 504 clicks and 16 installs points to weak creative relevance for the retargeted audience.

4. Facebook / iOS - App Promotion Campaign (Adjust)
   Spend: $988 | Installs: 118 | CPI: $8.37 (Meta-reported: $6.93, still 2x the benchmark)
   Nearly $1,000 spent for 118 installs while the Android equivalent drives 10x more volume at less than half the CPI. iOS unit economics are significantly worse.


SECTION 3 — RECOMMENDED ACTION PER UNDERPERFORMER
---------------------------------------------------

1. Meta / Cash Deposit
   Action: Pause immediately and audit the post-click funnel before any further spend.
   Rationale: A $53.75 CPI with 2,279 clicks and only 12 installs is a tracking or UX failure, not a targeting problem. Spending more before diagnosing is wasteful.

2. Meta / Re-engagement - Android
   Action: Pause and rebuild the audience definition, excluding users with any session in the past 30 days.
   Rationale: Six installs from a re-engagement campaign almost certainly means the audience overlap with active users is too high, making this budget structurally unspendable against the true target.

3. Meta / Re-targeting Campaign Android & iOS
   Action: Refresh creative and tighten the retargeting window to the past 7 days of app visitors only.
   Rationale: 22,257 impressions at $13.61 CPI indicates audience fatigue or wrong-window targeting; a tighter, more recent audience with new creative is the lowest-risk fix before cutting budget.

4. Facebook / iOS - App Promotion Campaign
   Action: Cut budget by 60% and reallocate to Apple / US - Brand - Exact, which delivers iOS installs at $3.31 CPI.
   Rationale: At $8.37 CPI (Adjust) the Meta iOS campaign costs 2.5x more than the Apple Search Ads brand campaign for the same platform; the budget works harder on Apple's own channel.
