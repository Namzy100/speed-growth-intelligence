Outreach Brief — Thinking Crypto (YouTube)
Segment: crypto-curious | Score: 63.6/100 | 194,000 followers
------------------------------------------------------------
Thinking Crypto has built a 194K-strong audience that lives squarely in the crypto-curious space — people who follow Bitcoin, Ethereum, DeFi, and Web3 closely enough to be interested, but who often still treat crypto as something to learn about rather than something they actually use day to day. That gap is exactly where Speed fits. The channel spends roughly 80% of its content on crypto and Bitcoin fundamentals, which means the audience already understands the "why" of Bitcoin; what they're missing is a simple, real-world way to put it to work. Speed gives them that.

For this audience, the strongest angle is simplicity and utility. The fact that Speed is a Lightning wallet that actually works for everyday payments — instant settlement, zero fees, and available on both iOS and Android — turns abstract Bitcoin enthusiasm into something tangible. Speed Stacks (earning sats on regular purchases) is a particularly good hook because it rewards the exact curiosity this audience already has.

Three talking points worth leading with: first, Speed bridges the gap between understanding Bitcoin and actually spending it via Lightning; second, the zero-fee, seconds-fast experience makes the Lightning Network concept concrete for viewers who've only heard about it theoretically; and third, stablecoin support (USDT, USDC) gives volatility-wary newcomers an on-ramp without forcing an all-in bet.

The recommended CTA is a hands-on walkthrough video — download Speed, fund a wallet, and make a first Lightning payment or earn sats on screen.

One thing to avoid: don't pitch this as a trading or speculation play. This audience responds to education and utility, not price hype.
