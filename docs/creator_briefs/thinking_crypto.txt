Outreach Brief — Thinking Crypto (YouTube)
Segment: crypto-curious | Score: 63.6/100 | 194,000 followers
------------------------------------------------------------
Thinking Crypto has built a 194K-strong audience that's genuinely curious about Bitcoin, blockchain, and where this whole space is heading — but curiosity isn't the same as usage. That gap is exactly where Speed fits. A lot of their viewers understand crypto conceptually but have never actually spent Bitcoin in the real world, and Speed is the bridge from "I follow the space" to "I use it daily."

Given their crypto-curious segment, the features to lead with are simplicity and everyday utility, not technical depth. Speed's zero-fee instant Lightning payments and the Speed Stacks rewards program (earning sats on normal purchases) make the abstract idea of using Bitcoin tangible. Stablecoin support also gives the value-stability skeptics in their audience a reason to try it without volatility anxiety.

Three talking points for the pitch: first, Speed turns the theory their channel teaches into something viewers can actually do — a natural extension of their educational content. Second, Speed Stacks gives them a concrete, demonstrable hook for a video, earning real sats on a coffee or a subscription on camera. Third, the iOS/Android availability means there's no intimidating setup barrier for a mainstream-leaning audience.

For the CTA, propose a single dedicated integration video where they walk through their first real-world Lightning payment using Speed, with a trackable link and Speed Stacks as the highlight.

One thing to avoid: don't pitch this as another speculative "crypto investment" play. Their audience is curious about utility — leaning into price hype or trading angles undercuts the everyday-payments story that makes Speed different.
