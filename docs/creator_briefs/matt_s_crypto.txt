Outreach Brief — Matt's Crypto (YouTube)
Segment: crypto-curious | Score: 66.6/100 | 137,000 followers
------------------------------------------------------------
Matt's Crypto sits right in the sweet spot for Speed. With 137K subscribers and an 11% engagement rate, his channel pulls in exactly the kind of crypto-curious viewer we want — people who are genuinely interested in Bitcoin and Ethereum but haven't necessarily figured out what to actually *do* with it day to day. His content leans heavily educational (those "knowledge" tags aren't an accident), which means his audience trusts him to explain things clearly rather than shill. That's a good foundation for a wallet that's all about practical utility.

Because this is a crypto-curious crowd, the messaging should center on simplicity and real-world usefulness rather than fee-savings or speed-for-traders angles. The features that matter most here: how easy Speed makes spending Bitcoin via Lightning, the zero-fee transactions, and Speed Stacks letting everyday purchases earn sats back. The stablecoin support is also worth a mention for viewers who like crypto but get spooked by volatility.

Three talking points for the pitch: first, Speed bridges the gap between "I understand Bitcoin" and "I actually use Bitcoin" — a natural next step for his viewers. Second, Lightning is the topic his audience will increasingly ask about, so covering it positions him ahead of the curve. Third, Speed Stacks gives him a tangible, demonstrable hook for a video — earning sats on a real purchase on camera.

Recommended CTA: invite him to a short call to scope a sponsored walkthrough or "how I actually spend Bitcoin" segment.

One thing to avoid: don't pitch this as a trading or investment tool. His audience wants to learn and use, not speculate.
