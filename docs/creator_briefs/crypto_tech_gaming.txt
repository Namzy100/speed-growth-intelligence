Outreach Brief — Crypto Tech Gaming (YouTube)
Segment: crypto-curious | Score: 64.6/100 | 88,600 followers
------------------------------------------------------------
Crypto Tech Gaming sits right in the sweet spot for Speed. Their audience is crypto-curious by nature — people who follow altcoin, DeFi, NFT, and play-to-earn content because they're genuinely interested in where Web3 is going, but who haven't necessarily made Bitcoin part of their daily life yet. That gap between curiosity and actual use is exactly what Speed closes. With an 80% crypto content mix and a solid 5.5% engagement rate, this is an audience already primed to hear that Bitcoin can be more than something you trade or speculate on.

Because they lean crypto-curious rather than remittance or iGaming, the messaging should center on simplicity and everyday utility, not fee math or gambling deposits. The features that land hardest here are instant Lightning payments settled in seconds, zero fees, and especially Speed Stacks — earning sats on regular purchases is a tangible, low-stakes way for a curious audience to start actually using Bitcoin. Stablecoin support (USDT, USDC) is a nice reassurance for anyone wary of volatility.

Three talking points worth leading with: first, Speed turns Bitcoin from a chart you watch into money you spend; second, Speed Stacks lets their viewers stack sats just by buying things they'd buy anyway — a natural bridge from play-to-earn thinking; third, it's available on iOS and Android and built for normal people, so there's no intimidating setup.

The CTA: invite them to a short call to scope a sponsored walkthrough or "I tried using Bitcoin for a week" style video.

Avoid framing Speed as a trading or investing tool — that misreads both the product and their audience.
