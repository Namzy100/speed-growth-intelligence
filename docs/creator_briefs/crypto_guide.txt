Outreach Brief — Crypto Guide (YouTube)
Segment: crypto-curious | Score: 49.3/100 | 28,600 followers
------------------------------------------------------------
Crypto Guide sits right in the sweet spot of our crypto-curious segment, which is exactly who Speed is built for. Their 28,600 YouTube followers are people leaning into Bitcoin and the surrounding lifestyle but still figuring out what to actually do with it day to day — and that gap between curiosity and real-world use is where Speed comes in. The hodl-and-lifestyle angle they lean on is useful here: Speed is what turns Bitcoin from something you just hold into something you can actually spend.

For this audience, the messaging should center on simplicity and utility rather than trading mechanics. The features that matter most are zero-fee Lightning payments that settle in seconds, the fact that it works on iOS and Android for normal people, stablecoin support for anyone nervous about volatility, and Speed Stacks letting them earn sats on everyday purchases — a natural reward hook for a crypto-curious crowd.

Three talking points worth pitching: first, Speed is the easy on-ramp from "I own some Bitcoin" to "I can pay with it," which fits their educational, explainer style. Second, Speed Stacks gives them a concrete, demoable reward to show their audience earning sats. Third, stablecoin support answers the volatility objection their crypto-curious viewers will raise.

I'd recommend a CTA built around a sponsored walkthrough video — download, set up, make a real payment on camera — since this is their first brand deal and a hands-on demo suits both their content and our utility story.

One thing to avoid: don't pitch this as a trading or speculation tool. Keep it about spending and everyday use.
