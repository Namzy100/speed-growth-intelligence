Outreach Brief — Crypto Warehouse (YouTube)
Segment: crypto-curious | Score: 62.6/100 | 7,010 followers
------------------------------------------------------------
Crypto Warehouse sits squarely in the crypto-curious lane, which is exactly where Speed wins. Their channel covers altcoins, ETH, Bitcoin, NFTs, and the broader "how do I actually get into this" investing conversation — and that audience is full of people who understand crypto conceptually but haven't found a reason to use it in daily life. Speed gives them that reason: a Bitcoin Lightning wallet that works for real purchases, not just speculation.

For this audience, the hook is simplicity and utility, not fee math or trading angles. Lead with how Speed makes Bitcoin feel usable — instant Lightning payments that settle in seconds, zero fees, and a clean iOS/Android experience that doesn't require you to be a power user. Speed Stacks is the standout here: earning sats on everyday purchases is a tangible "why bother" answer for someone curious but not yet committed. Stablecoin support (USDT, USDC) is a nice secondary point for viewers nervous about volatility.

Three talking points for the pitch: first, Speed turns the theory their channel teaches into something viewers can actually do today. Second, Speed Stacks gives them a fresh content angle — earning Bitcoin by spending, not buying. Third, the stablecoin option lets cautious viewers participate without diving into price swings.

Recommended CTA: invite them to do a hands-on "I tried using Bitcoin for everyday payments" walkthrough video, with a tracked download link and a Speed Stacks demo.

One thing to avoid: don't frame Speed as a trading or investing tool. This audience already gets that elsewhere — Speed's edge is spending, not speculating.
