Outreach Brief — Discover Crypto (YouTube)
Segment: crypto-curious | Score: 58.1/100 | 1,390,000 followers
------------------------------------------------------------
Discover Crypto sits right in the sweet spot for Speed. Their 1.39M subscribers are crypto-curious by nature — people who follow altcoin breakdowns, Bitcoin explainers, and trading content because they're interested but still figuring out how this stuff actually fits into their lives. That's exactly the audience Speed is built for: folks who get the concept of Bitcoin but haven't found a reason to use it day to day. Speed gives them that reason.

For this segment, lead with simplicity and utility, not fee-saving spreadsheets or transaction-speed benchmarks. The hooks that'll land are how easy Speed is to set up on iOS or Android, how it turns Bitcoin into something you can actually spend, and Speed Stacks letting their viewers earn sats on everyday purchases — a tangible reward that bridges "I bought some Bitcoin" and "I use Bitcoin."

Three talking points worth raising in the pitch: first, Speed converts crypto knowledge into crypto behavior — a natural next step for an audience that already understands the theory. Second, Speed Stacks gives the channel a genuinely fun, demonstrable feature to show on camera rather than just talk about. Third, this would be their first brand deal, so position Speed as a clean, credible launch partner rather than one of many.

For the CTA, suggest a dedicated segment or short integration walking viewers through downloading Speed and earning their first sats with Stacks, tied to a trackable link.

One thing to avoid: don't frame this around trading or price speculation. Speed is a payments and utility play, and leaning into trading would attract the wrong intent and undercut the message.
