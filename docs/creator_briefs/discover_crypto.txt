Outreach Brief — Discover Crypto (YouTube)
Segment: crypto-curious | Score: 58.1/100 | 1,390,000 followers
------------------------------------------------------------
Discover Crypto sits right in the sweet spot for Speed. With 1.39M subscribers who follow them precisely because they're learning their way through altcoins, Bitcoin, and Ethereum, this is an audience that's curious and engaged but not necessarily deep into the practical, everyday side of crypto yet. They understand what Bitcoin is — they just haven't been shown a clean reason to actually use it. That gap is exactly what Speed fills.

Because this is a crypto-curious crowd, lead with simplicity and real-world utility rather than fee tables or technical Lightning details. The hook is that Speed turns Bitcoin from something you watch charts about into something you can actually spend. Speed Stacks is the standout feature here — earning sats on everyday purchases gives a learning-focused audience a low-stakes, tangible reason to get started. Pair that with the iOS/Android availability and the "built for normal people" framing.

For the pitch, three angles worth leading with: first, that Speed bridges the exact gap their channel creates — viewers who get the theory but want a first practical step. Second, that Speed Stacks rewards everyday spending, which makes for genuinely watchable demo content. Third, that stablecoin support (USDT, USDC) gives the more cautious crypto-curious viewer a stable on-ramp without the volatility fear.

Recommended CTA: a walkthrough or "I tried using Bitcoin for a week" style video where they set up Speed live and earn their first sats, with a tracked link in the description.

One thing to avoid: don't frame Speed as an investment or trading play. This is a payments and utility story, not another altcoin pick.
