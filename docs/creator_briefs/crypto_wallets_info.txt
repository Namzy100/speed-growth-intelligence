Outreach Brief — Crypto Wallets Info (YouTube)
Segment: crypto-curious | Score: 54.6/100 | 22,300 followers
------------------------------------------------------------
Crypto Wallets Info has built an audience around exactly the kind of educational, explainer-style content that maps well to Speed's crypto-curious segment. Their viewers are clearly interested in blockchain and DeFi but are still in the learning phase — people who want to understand crypto before they actually use it. That's the gap Speed fills. We're not asking their audience to become traders or yield farmers; we're showing them a Bitcoin wallet that works like a normal payments app, which is a natural next step after the "what is a wallet" content they're already making.

For this audience, lean into simplicity and everyday utility rather than technical depth. The strongest features to highlight are the zero-fee instant Lightning payments, the iOS/Android availability that makes onboarding painless, and Speed Stacks, since earning sats on regular purchases is an easy, tangible hook for someone curious but hesitant.

Three talking points for the pitch: first, Speed turns abstract Lightning concepts they've explained into something their viewers can actually try in minutes. Second, the stablecoin support (USDT, USDC) gives nervous newcomers a stable-value option, which addresses the volatility fear that holds crypto-curious people back. Third, Speed Stacks gives them a genuinely fresh angle for a video — earning Bitcoin while spending, not just buying and holding.

A good CTA would be a dedicated wallet walkthrough or first-payment demo video with a tracked signup link, since their content style already suits tutorials.

One thing to avoid: don't frame this as an investment or trading play. Their audience responds to learning and utility, and price-speculation messaging would feel off-brand and erode trust.
