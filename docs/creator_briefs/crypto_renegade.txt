Outreach Brief — Crypto Renegade (YouTube)
Segment: crypto-curious | Score: 46.9/100 | 22,100 followers
------------------------------------------------------------
Crypto Renegade sits squarely in the crypto-curious space, which is exactly where Speed lives. Their audience leans into the "sociology of crypto" angle — people fascinated by Bitcoin's place in everyday life, not just price charts. That's a natural bridge to Speed, because the whole pitch is that Bitcoin can actually be used to pay for things, not just held and theorized about. With a 12% engagement rate, this is a smaller but genuinely curious audience that's still figuring out where to start, and that's the gap Speed fills.

For this segment, lead with simplicity and utility. The features that matter most here are zero-fee Lightning payments, settlement in seconds, and Speed Stacks — earning sats on everyday purchases is a concrete, relatable hook for someone who's "crypto-curious but not sure where to start." Stablecoin support is a nice secondary point for the cautious viewer who wants stability before going full Bitcoin.

Three talking points to pitch: first, Speed turns Bitcoin from an abstract idea into something you actually spend, which fits their lifestyle-and-tech framing perfectly. Second, it's built for normal people — iOS and Android, no jargon required — so it's an easy recommendation for a curious-but-hesitant audience. Third, Speed Stacks gives viewers a reason to try it today, not someday.

For the CTA, propose a hands-on walkthrough video where they download Speed, make a real Lightning payment, and show the experience start to finish.

One thing to avoid: don't frame this as an investment or trading play. Their audience is exploring, not speculating — keep it about utility.
