Outreach Brief — Passive Income Network (YouTube)
Segment: crypto-curious | Score: 58.1/100 | 5,790 followers
------------------------------------------------------------
Passive Income Network sits right in the crypto-curious sweet spot, and that's exactly who Speed is built for. Their audience already follows Bitcoin, Ethereum, and DeFi content for the "knowledge" angle — they're learning, not necessarily transacting yet. That gap is the opportunity. These viewers understand the why of crypto but haven't been shown a practical, everyday use for it. Speed bridges that.

The features that'll land here are the ones tied to simplicity and real utility. For an audience that values passive income and smart money habits, Speed Stacks is the obvious anchor — earning sats back on purchases they're already making fits their whole content thesis. Pair that with zero-fee Lightning payments and stablecoin support, and you've got a story about Bitcoin actually doing something, not just sitting in a wallet waiting to moon.

Three talking points for the pitch: first, Speed turns Bitcoin from a "someday" investment into something usable today, which extends their educational content into action. Second, Speed Stacks aligns perfectly with their passive-income brand — a way to accumulate sats without trading or risk. Third, the iOS/Android availability means their less technical viewers can actually follow along, not bounce at a complicated setup.

For the CTA, propose a dedicated walkthrough video where they set up Speed live and make a real purchase using Speed Stacks, with a tracked signup link for their audience.

One thing to avoid: don't lean into price speculation or "get rich" framing. This audience responds to knowledge and utility, and hype messaging would undercut the credibility their channel is built on.
