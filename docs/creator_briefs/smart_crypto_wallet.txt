Outreach Brief — SMART CRYPTO WALLET (YouTube)
Segment: crypto-curious | Score: 68.6/100 | 28,600 followers
------------------------------------------------------------
SMART CRYPTO WALLET sits right in the sweet spot for Speed. Their channel is built around helping crypto-curious viewers figure out where to actually start, and that's exactly the audience we struggle to reach with most partners. With a 10.4% engagement rate and roughly 2,966 weighted engaged viewers, this is a small but genuinely active audience that trusts the creator's recommendations — and crucially, they have no prior brand deals, so a Speed partnership won't feel like the tenth sponsorship slot.

Because their viewers are interested in Bitcoin but still feel intimidated by it, the angle here is simplicity and everyday utility, not trading or speculation. The features that land: zero-fee instant Lightning payments that settle in seconds, stablecoin support (USDT, USDC) for anyone nervous about volatility, and Speed Stacks letting them earn sats on normal purchases. That turns "I bought some Bitcoin" into "I actually use it."

Three talking points for the pitch: first, Speed makes Bitcoin something you spend, not just hold — a natural next step for their "where do I start" audience. Second, the stablecoin option answers the volatility fear their viewers raise constantly. Third, Speed Stacks gives a concrete, low-stakes reason to download and try it today.

For the CTA, ask them to walk through a real purchase on Speed on camera and drop a tracked download link in the description — a demo beats a read-out every time.

One thing to avoid: don't lean into price predictions, "get rich" framing, or NFT hype. Their audience wants confidence and clarity, and that tone would undercut Speed's everyday-utility positioning.
