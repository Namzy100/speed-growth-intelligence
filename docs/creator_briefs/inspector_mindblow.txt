Outreach Brief — Inspector Mindblow (YouTube)
Segment: crypto-curious | Score: 63.1/100 | 91,100 followers
------------------------------------------------------------
Inspector Mindblow sits right in the sweet spot for Speed. His audience is crypto-curious — people who watch his channel because they're interested in ETH, NFTs, trading, and the broader web3 world, but who aren't necessarily hardcore Bitcoiners yet. That "curious but cautious" mindset is exactly who Speed is built for. With a 67% crypto content mix and a strong 8.5% engagement rate, he's reaching an audience that's already leaning in but still looking for the practical "okay, but what do I actually *do* with this" answer. Speed is that answer.

Because his viewers are exploring rather than committed, lead with simplicity and real-world utility, not technical Lightning depth. The hooks that land here: Speed works like a normal payments app on iOS and Android, you can actually spend Bitcoin on everyday purchases, and Speed Stacks lets you earn sats back while you do it. Stablecoin support (USDT, USDC) is also worth a mention for the trading-minded part of his crowd who want stable value between moves.

Three talking points for the pitch: first, Speed bridges the gap between "owning crypto" and "using crypto," which is the natural next step for his curious audience. Second, zero fees and instant settlement make it a genuinely better daily-use product, not just another wallet. Third, Speed Stacks gives him a concrete, demonstrable feature to show on camera — earning sats on a real purchase.

For the CTA, propose a dedicated walkthrough or first-impressions video where he sets up Speed live and makes a real payment.

One thing to avoid: don't pitch this as a speculative investment or "get rich" play — his audience trusts him for exploration, and a hype angle would undercut that.
