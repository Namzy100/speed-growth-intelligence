Outreach Brief — Inspector Mindblow (YouTube)
Segment: crypto-curious | Score: 63.1/100 | 91,100 followers
------------------------------------------------------------
Inspector Mindblow sits right in the sweet spot we look for in a crypto-curious partner. With 91K subs and a content mix that leans heavily into crypto, ETH, NFTs, trading and broader web3 culture, their audience is already intrigued by Bitcoin but probably hasn't found a clean way to actually use it day to day. That gap is exactly what Speed fills, and the channel's 8.5% engagement rate tells us this is a viewer base that listens and acts when the creator recommends something.

Because their crowd is crypto-aware but not necessarily deep into payments, the messaging should lead with simplicity and real utility, not technical Lightning jargon. The hooks that matter here: zero-fee instant Lightning payments that settle in seconds, stablecoin support (USDT/USDC) for anyone who wants to dodge volatility, and Speed Stacks letting them earn sats on everyday spending.

Three talking points worth building the pitch around: first, this is Bitcoin you can actually spend, bridging the gap between holding crypto and using it; second, the gaming and strategy angle in their niche tags pairs naturally with the "earn sats while you spend" reward mechanic; third, Speed works on iOS and Android for normal people, so it's an easy, low-friction recommendation that won't alienate the less technical part of their audience.

For the CTA, I'd propose a dedicated walkthrough video where they set up Speed live and make a real Lightning payment, tied to a tracked sign-up link.

One thing to avoid: don't frame Speed as a trading or investment play. Their viewers already get plenty of that elsewhere, and it muddies the "spend and use" message.
