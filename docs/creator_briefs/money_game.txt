------------------------------------------------------------
MONEY GAME sits right in the crypto-curious sweet spot, and that's exactly the audience Speed is built for. The story is that Bitcoin can move from abstract concept to something you actually use day to day. Three talking points worth raising: first, this is a natural next step for an audience that already understands the theory and wants the practical side. Second, the stablecoin support (USDT, USDC) gives nervous newcomers a way in without the volatility fear. Third, Speed Stacks turns "learning about Bitcoin" into "earning a little Bitcoin," which is a satisfying payoff for an educational channel.

For the CTA, propose a single integrated demo video where they walk through setting up Speed and making a first payment live on camera.

One thing to avoid: don't frame this as a trading or get-rich angle — it'll clash with their knowledge-first, society-focused tone.
