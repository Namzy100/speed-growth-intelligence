Hey team — flagging "cryptocurrency for beginners" as a moderate but worth-it candidate for outreach. The name says it all: this channel exists to help people take their first steps into Bitcoin, and that's exactly the crypto-curious audience Speed is built for. Their content leans heavily educational (around 80% crypto/Bitcoin), with a lighter, humorous, entertainment-driven tone running through it. That mix matters — their viewers aren't hardcore traders, they're curious newcomers who need things explained simply. That's our wedge.

The Speed features to lead with here are simplicity and real-world utility, not technical depth. For an audience still figuring out what Satoshi even built, the pitch is "here's Bitcoin you can actually use today" — instant payments settled in seconds, zero Lightning fees, and Speed Stacks letting them earn sats on everyday purchases. Stablecoin support (USDT, USDC) is a nice secondary mention for the ones nervous about volatility.

Three talking points for the pitch: first, Speed turns abstract crypto theory into something their viewers can hold and spend, which fits a beginner channel perfectly. Second, the Stacks rewards angle gives them a genuinely fun, demonstrable hook for an entertainment-style creator. Third, this is their first brand deal, so we can offer a clean, low-friction starter partnership and grow from there.

Recommended CTA: propose a single sponsored walkthrough video — downloading Speed and making a first real Lightning payment on camera, with a trackable link.

One thing to avoid: don't lean on trading, price speculation, or "get rich" framing. That betrays the beginner-trust this channel is built on.
