Outreach Brief — Crypto Daily Trade Signals (YouTube)
Segment: crypto-curious | Score: 59.1/100 | 83,100 followers
------------------------------------------------------------
Crypto Daily Trade Signals sits in an interesting spot for us. Their 83K subscribers come for trading signals and crypto education, which means they're already past the "what is Bitcoin" hurdle but still firmly crypto-curious when it comes to actually *using* Bitcoin day to day. That gap is exactly where Speed lives. Their audience knows how to read a chart, but most of them have never spent a sat on anything real. We can be the bridge from speculation to utility.

For this crowd, the simplicity and utility angle does the heavy lifting. Lead with Speed Stacks — earning sats on everyday purchases is a tangible, relatable hook for people who already think in terms of stacking. Pair it with instant Lightning payments and the stablecoin support, since a trading audience understands the value of moving between BTC and stable value without friction.

Three talking points for the pitch: first, their viewers already hold or trade Bitcoin, so the "I have BTC, now what?" question writes itself. Second, Speed Stacks rewards turn everyday spending into accumulation, which speaks their language. Third, zero-fee Lightning and instant settlement show Bitcoin actually working as money, not just an asset to flip.

For the CTA, propose a dedicated walkthrough video — downloading Speed, making a first Lightning payment, and showing Stacks rewards land in real time, with a tracked link in the description.

One thing to avoid: don't frame Speed as an investment or trading tool. This isn't about price action or returns — leaning that way invites compliance headaches and misses the point entirely.
