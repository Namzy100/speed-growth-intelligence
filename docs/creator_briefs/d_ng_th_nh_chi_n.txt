Outreach Brief — Dương Thành Chiến (TikTok)
Segment: crypto-curious | Score: 60.1/100 | 121,800 followers
------------------------------------------------------------
Dương Thành Chiến has built a 121.8K-strong TikTok audience around Bitcoin, ETH, and trading content, which means his followers already understand crypto at a conceptual level but may not have made the jump to actually using it day-to-day. That gap is exactly where Speed fits. His crypto-curious audience knows what Bitcoin is; they just need a reason and an easy on-ramp to treat it as money rather than a chart they watch. With a 6.1% engagement rate and roughly 7,500 weighted engaged viewers per post, his reach is modest but his audience is paying attention.

Because his viewers lean trader and crypto-curious rather than remittance or gaming, the messaging should center on simplicity and real-world utility. The strongest hooks are zero-fee instant Lightning payments, stablecoin support in USDT and USDC for anyone wanting to sit in stable value between trades, and Speed Stacks letting them earn sats on everyday purchases.

For the pitch, lead with three points: first, Speed turns the Bitcoin his audience already follows into something they can spend in seconds with no fees; second, stablecoin support gives his trading-minded followers a familiar tool for managing volatility; and third, Speed Stacks rewards everyday spending, which gives him a fresh, non-price-speculation angle for content.

The call to action: invite him to a short call to walk through the app and discuss a first sponsored video, ideally a hands-on demo sending a real payment.

One thing to avoid: don't frame Speed as an investment or trading platform. It's a payments wallet, and overpromising returns would mislead his audience and misrepresent the product.
