Outreach Brief — Torugão Crypto (YouTube)
Segment: iGaming | Score: 22.3/100 | 26,200 followers
------------------------------------------------------------
Torugão Crypto sits at an interesting intersection for us. His channel blends crypto with play-to-earn gaming, RPGs, and strategy content, which means his 26,200 subscribers already understand digital assets but engage with them through the lens of gaming and earning. That's our iGaming segment with a crypto-literate twist — people who care about getting value in and out of games quickly and don't need a Bitcoin 101 explainer.

Given that mix, the instant-payment angle is what to lead with. For an audience that thinks in terms of play-to-earn mechanics and in-game economies, the hook is moving funds in and out in seconds with zero Lightning fees — no waiting on withdrawals, no watching fees eat into earnings. Speed Stacks is a natural secondary point since earning sats on everyday spending mirrors the earn-while-you-play mindset his viewers already enjoy.

Three talking points for the pitch: first, instant deposits and withdrawals settled in seconds, framed around keeping gaming cash flow fast; second, zero Lightning fees, which protects play-to-earn returns; third, stablecoin support (USDT, USDC) for anyone who wants to lock in value between sessions rather than ride volatility.

For the call to action, I'd propose a single integrated video segment where he walks through funding a wallet and making a fast withdrawal live — low commitment for a first-time brand partner with no prior deals, and easy to measure.

One thing to avoid: don't pitch Speed as a gambling or betting tool. Keep it about fast, low-cost payments around gaming, not promoting wagering itself.
