Outreach Brief — Crypto Casey (YouTube)
Segment: crypto-curious | Score: 60.7/100 | 677,000 followers
------------------------------------------------------------
Crypto Casey has built a 677K-strong audience that's exactly who Speed is trying to reach: crypto-curious people who want to understand Bitcoin but haven't been handed a practical reason to actually use it. Her content leans educational—she explains altcoins, Ethereum, and blockchain fundamentals to people still finding their footing—which makes her a natural bridge to a wallet that turns Bitcoin from a concept into something you spend. The 12% she already dedicates to fintech and payments tells us she's comfortable connecting crypto to real-world money movement, and that's the lane we want to occupy.

For her audience, the hook is simplicity and utility, not yield or speculation. The features worth leading with are zero-fee Lightning payments, instant settlement in seconds, and Speed Stacks, which lets everyday purchases earn sats back. Stablecoin support (USDT, USDC) is a nice secondary point for the segment of her viewers who like Bitcoin but get nervous about volatility.

Three talking points for the pitch: first, Speed answers the "okay, now what?" question her tutorials naturally raise—it's the practical next step after learning. Second, it's built for normal people on iOS and Android, not just power users, which matches her teaching tone. Third, Speed Stacks gives her a concrete, demonstrable reason to keep using it on camera over time.

The call to action: propose a hands-on walkthrough video where she sets up Speed and makes a real purchase live, ideally with a trackable signup link.

One thing to avoid: don't frame this as an investment or earning play—that undercuts her educational credibility and misreads her audience.
