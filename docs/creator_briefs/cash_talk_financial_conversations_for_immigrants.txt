Outreach Brief — Cash Talk: Financial Conversations for Immigrants (YouTube)
Segment: remittance | Score: 42.0/100 | 5,780 followers
------------------------------------------------------------
Cash Talk has built something genuinely valuable: a YouTube channel that walks immigrants through real financial decisions, with remittance sitting right at the center of those conversations. That's exactly the audience Speed wants to reach. People sending money home internationally feel the cost of every transfer, and a creator who's already trusted to talk honestly about money is in a strong position to introduce a better option. The engagement quality here is high even if the channel is still small, which matters more for this kind of partnership than raw follower count.

The hook for this audience is fees, plain and simple. Speed's Lightning transactions carry zero fees and settle in seconds, which is a sharp contrast to the cut that traditional remittance services and wire transfers take on every send. For viewers who don't want exposure to Bitcoin's volatility, stablecoin support in USDT and USDC means they can send stable value without the price-swing anxiety.

Three talking points worth leading with: first, how much a family actually loses to remittance fees over a year and what zero-fee transfers would save them; second, that money arrives in seconds, not days, which matters when someone back home needs it now; third, that Speed is built for everyday people on iOS and Android, not just crypto insiders.

For the call to action, propose a single honest walkthrough video where they send a real remittance using Speed and show the actual cost difference.

One thing to avoid: don't frame this as a crypto investment pitch. This audience cares about sending money home, not speculating.
